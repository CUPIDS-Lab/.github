We are **a laboratory** for teaching data science in the public interest, **a clinic**
connecting Colorado's watchdogs to technical capacity, and **a network** preserving the
evidence democratic accountability depends on. Based in Boulder at the University of
Colorado, we bring together students, faculty, journalists, technologists, public
servants, and residents to do data work that serves the public good.

## 🧭 Mission

Public interest data science puts **transparency, accountability, and justice** at the
forefront of data work — drawing on the public-service traditions of journalism, law,
and accounting. We acquire, document, and defend the public datasets that communities,
reporters, and courts rely on, and we help people make sense of them in the open.

## 💡 What we value

- **Public interest first** — we prioritize transparency over efficiency and justice over profitability.
- **Data is not neutral.** It is shaped by human choices, power, and context, so it must be interpreted, explained, debated, and held accountable in public.
- **Rigor & ethics** — rigorous inquiry, democratic accountability, and ethical conduct in everything we ship.
- **Open & durable** — open methods and durable archives that outlast any single link, agency, or administration.
- **Community over the clock** — pro-bono, collaborative work with and for Colorado's journalists, lawyers, and nonprofits.

## 🛠️ What we do

| Pillar | Description |
| --- | --- |
| 📰 **Research Newsroom** | Cross-disciplinary teams ship a monthly dispatch — explainers, dataset guides, and original investigations. |
| 🗄️ **Data Infrastructure** | We acquire, clean, document, and archive critical public datasets in durable, open repositories. |
| 🆘 **Community Help Desk** | Pro-bono data support for journalists, lawyers, and nonprofits across Colorado. |

## 🧰 Resources & tooling

Reusable tools we build so that liberating and stewarding public data is repeatable, documented, and open.

| Repository | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| [**data-liberation-skill**](https://github.com/CUPIDS-Lab/data-liberation-skill) | Agent skill for orchestrating data-liberation projects — acquiring, cleaning, documenting, and archiving public datasets end to end. |
| [**data-liberation-template**](https://github.com/CUPIDS-Lab/data-liberation-template) | Python project template the data-liberation skill scaffolds for each new civic-data liberation effort. |
| [**data-project-skill**](https://github.com/CUPIDS-Lab/data-project-skill) | Skill for architecting repositories, pipelines, and documentation that support open and inclusive data collaborations. |
